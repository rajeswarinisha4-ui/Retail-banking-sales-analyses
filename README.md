# Retail-banking-sales-analyses
Retail bank sales analysis project focused on evaluating customer transactions, revenue trends, and product performance using Excel and Power BI
# Objective
To identify key insights such as top-performing cities, revenue contribution, customer trends, and product performance to support business decision-making.
# Tools Used
Excel (Data Cleaning & Preparation)
Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
# Key Insights
Identified top 5 cities contributing highest revenue
Analyzed monthly revenue trends
Evaluated customer purchasing behavior
analyse total revenue by channel
Calculated average ticket size to understand spending patterns
